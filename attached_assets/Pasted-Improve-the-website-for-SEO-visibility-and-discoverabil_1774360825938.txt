Improve the website for SEO, visibility, and discoverability on search engines.

This is a professional surveying company website for Oak Surveys based in Lagos, Nigeria. The goal is to make the website more likely to appear on Google searches and attract potential clients.

SEO STRUCTURE

1. Add proper SEO metadata for every page:

Homepage title:
Oak Surveys – Professional Land & Engineering Surveying Services in Lagos, Nigeria Since 1974

Homepage description:
Oak Surveys is a professional surveying and geospatial services firm based in Lagos, Nigeria. Established in 1974, we provide land surveying, engineering surveys, hydrographic surveys, and mapping services.

About page title:
About Oak Surveys | Established Surveying Firm in Lagos Since 1974

Services page title:
Surveying Services in Lagos | Land, Engineering & Mapping | Oak Surveys

Contact page title:
Contact Oak Surveys | Professional Surveyors in Lagos, Nigeria

Ensure all pages have unique and properly structured meta titles and descriptions.

---

2. Improve on-page SEO content:

Ensure the following keywords appear naturally across the website:
- surveying services in Lagos
- land surveying Nigeria
- engineering surveys
- hydrographic surveys
- geospatial mapping
- surveying company Lagos Nigeria

Do not keyword stuff. Keep it natural and professional.

---

3. Add a structured FAQ section:

Add a section titled “Frequently Asked Questions” on the homepage or services page.

Include questions such as:
- What surveying services does Oak Surveys provide?
- Where is Oak Surveys located?
- How can I request a survey?
- What types of projects do you handle?

Provide clear, concise answers.

---

4. Improve image SEO:

Ensure all images have descriptive alt text.

Examples:
- "Surveyor conducting land survey using GPS equipment in Lagos"
- "Topographic mapping project by Oak Surveys in Nigeria"

Avoid generic alt text like “image1”.

---

5. Add structured data (advanced but important):

Implement basic schema markup for:
- Organization (Oak Surveys)
- Local business (location in Lagos, Nigeria)

Include:
- company name
- address
- phone number (if available)
- website URL

---

6. Generate a sitemap:

Create a sitemap.xml that includes:
- homepage
- about page
- services page
- contact page

Ensure it is accessible at:
oaksurveys.com/sitemap.xml

---

7. Improve internal linking:

Ensure pages link to each other properly:
- homepage links to services and contact
- services page links to contact
- about page links to services

---

8. Add strong location signals:

Ensure Lagos, Nigeria appears clearly in:
- homepage
- contact page
- footer

Add full address in footer:
No. 66 Eleshin Street, Obalende, Lagos, Nigeria

---

9. Improve performance:

- optimize images for faster loading
- ensure the site loads quickly
- avoid unnecessary heavy elements

---

10. Final SEO polish:

- ensure headings follow proper hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- ensure content is readable and well structured
- ensure no duplicate titles or descriptions

FINAL GOAL

The website should:
- rank better on Google
- clearly communicate location (Lagos, Nigeria)
- attract potential clients searching for surveying services
- look professional and credible

Do not redesign the website. Focus on SEO, structure, and visibility improvements only.
